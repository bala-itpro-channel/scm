About Us

Founded in 2011, by Kiran Bhavsar & Mohan Chelliah, 2 Highly experienced Pioneers in Millwork with over 30 years experience for each of them. With highly experienced craftsmen, Sonoma Custom Millwork INC. delivers high-quality, custom fixtures for High end retail, commercial spaces, condominiums, hotels, Restaurants and custom residences. Based in TORONTO, Ontario, our workshop blends traditional craftsmanship with advanced CNC technology to create precision-built, durable fixtures. For Clients through out Canada and USA – Mainly New York, Houston, Miami, Atlanta, Boston, Washinton DC, for Our Repeat Clients & Customers.

Services

We provide end-to-end custom millwork and retail fixture solutions, seamlessly integrating design, manufacturing, installation, and distribution under one roof. From concept to completion, our team delivers precision craftsmanship, innovative design, and reliable execution for commercial, retail, and residential spaces across North America.

MANUFACTURING

Proudly made in Canada, our manufacturing process is built on precision, craftsmanship, and full accountability from start to finish. Operating from our state-of-the-art facility, we combine advanced manufacturing technology with skilled workmanship to produce high-quality custom millwork and retail fixtures tailored to your exact specifications. Our experienced team delivers custom architectural millwork solutions with exceptional attention to detail in every project. This hands-on approach ensures strict quality control and consistently reliable results for commercial, retail, and residential projects—regardless of size or complexity.

DESIGN

Ready to elevate your retail space? Whether you’re starting from scratch or refining an existing concept, we collaborate closely with you to create innovative retail fixture design and custom millwork solutions that bring your vision to life. We work exclusively with certified designers and architects, ensuring every project meets the highest standards of commercial interior design, space planning, and architectural precision, while maintaining full compliance with industry regulations.

INSTALLATION

Our installation team understands every detail of your fixtures—because we built them. We provide seamless, professional fixture installation and millwork installation services across Canada and the United States, ensuring everything is installed correctly and performs as intended. We also accommodate on-site adjustments and last-minute changes, ensuring your custom retail fixtures and millwork systems meet your exact requirements, even on tight timelines.
